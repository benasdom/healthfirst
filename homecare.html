Why is home care important

HFCRA is an agency that specialises in the training and recruitment of all health care professionals especially nurses to an extensive range of clients; consisting of hospitals, domiciliary care services to private patients and to those referred by various hospitals and social services About Us HFCRA is a specialist homecare and recruitment agency which supplies homes, hospitals and other health care clients in Ghana with highly trained nurses, care assistants, support workers and more . HFCRA stands for perfection and quality in both our services and staffing. Our staffs are taken through a special training to make sure they offer the best service in their work regardless of the quantum of work
Home-care services
The services HFCRA runs include • HOMECARE (Live-in) We provide specially trained personnel for client of various needs : social companionship, palliative care, specialist care, support with activities of daily living, respite care, night care, discharge from hospital. Our Live-in staff stays with the patient and are able to see to the patient’s needs 24 hours within a day. • HOMECARE (live-out) We provide specially trained personnel for client of various needs : social companionship, palliative care, specialist care, support with activities of daily living, respite care, night care, discharge from hospital. Our live-out staff unlike the live in , comes to the patient’s home daily and perform his or her duties diligently and closed at the end of the day . This will be most ideal for patients who will not need health care services at night. • STAFF EMPLOYMENT We employ health professionals who have the various qualifications and retrain them to be well equipped before matching them to patients to care for. • RECRUITMENT FOR HOSPITALS We recruit well trained and experienced professionals for hospitals, clinics and any health centres.
